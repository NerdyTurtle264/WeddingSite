THE VENUE

The South Mountain Inn is a historic Bed and Breakfast twenty minutes from Reading, PA. It sits on seven lovely acres of woodland mountains and was a scenic spot for both the ceremony and guest accommodations.

The Leadup

The guests arrived the night before the wedding. After settling in, the families unpacked. After only two minor disasters: a busted case of root beer sending stickiness and glass shards all over the dining room and the bride’s shoe breaking sending her falling up the stairs while carrying an heirloom cake stand and only getting mildly bruised, the families had dinner and settled in for a long day.
