Alison and Johnny

After eight and a half years together, Ali and Johnny were married on Saturday September 25, 2021 at 11:15am. They wed at The South Mountain Inn in Wernersville, PA. Photographs were taken by the groom’s mother and sister: Mary Boden and Chloe Ellenberger. Professional photography was provided by Emily Ziegler of photosbyemilly.com. More photos of the day can be found there. The realities of marrying during a pandemic mean that many of those they cherish could not attend their ceremony. The intended solution to this was to have the ceremony broadcast over livestream, however, as many of you experienced, the technical realities of the situation made that untenable. Thankfully, Mary and Chloe were at the ready to record the days events. Use the tabs at the top of the screen to see this special day for the Ashens, Bodens and Lashinskys.
